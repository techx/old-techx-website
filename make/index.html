18 hours

A hardware-based hackathon promoting innovation that is geared towards those who are excited and passionate about designing and building. Teams of three to four will compete first in an 18 hour prototyping competition. Top teams will then return to MIT two weeks later to manufacture and iterate their completed prototypes and to bring their designs to life.

FAQ

Who can attend?
Any college student within the Boston area!

What should I bring?
Just your student ID and laptop for hacking!

How big should teams be? Can I sign up alone?
Teams should be groups of 3-4 people. If you don't have a team, don't worry! You may register and look for teammates after registration.

How much will it cost?
This event is completely free!

What should I make?
Whatever your heart desires. We hope this hackathon will inspire and motivate you

I have a question not listed. Who do I ask?
You can get in touch with us via email or facebook!
